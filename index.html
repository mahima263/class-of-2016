The Batch Message

Coming back to the people who made it unforgettable

Ten years ago, we were walking into lecture halls, hostels, labs, clubs and wings - not knowing that IITK would give us much more than a degree.

It gave us wingies, fundae, nightouts, GPLs, treats, campus walks, OAT evenings, New SAC rooftops, Hall canteen conversations, Galaxy enthu, Antaragni madness, Techkriti hustle, Udghosh energy — and friendships that somehow survived placements, cities, time zones and adult life.

From last-minute notes before exams to endless bakar after midnight, from "chaapna hai" to "chill maar", from CS office to Hall rooms, from department hangouts to the final campus walk — we have carried a piece of IITK everywhere we went.

This 10-year reunion is not just about coming back to campus. It is about coming back to the people who made those four years unforgettable.

Let's return to where many of our best stories began — our second home, IIT Kanpur.

Travel & stay

Everything you need to get back to campus comfortably.

Reaching IITK

  • IIT Kanpur is located approximately 17 km from Kanpur city centre.
  • Air: Kanpur Airport (KNU) ~25 km; Lucknow Airport (LKO) ~90 km from campus.
  • Rail: Kanpur Central (CNB) is the nearest major railway station.
  • Metro: Kanpur Metro connects within the city; taxis & auto-rickshaws available for onward travel to campus.
  • Road: App-based cabs & taxis are easily available from airports, railway stations and from campus too.

Where to Stay

  • Accommodation for registered guests will be communicated separately.
  • Limited campus accommodation may be available, subject to approval and availability.
  • Participants are advised to confirm accommodation arrangements in advance.

Good to Know

  • Carry a valid government and alumni card for campus entry.
  • Follow IIT Kanpur security and visitor guidelines during your visit.
  • November weather is generally pleasant; light woollens recommended for evenings.
